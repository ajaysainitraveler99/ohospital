About Shiv Hospital

Providing exceptional healthcare services since 2005 with a commitment to excellence and patient-centered care.

Founded in 2005, Shiv Hospital has grown to become a trusted name in healthcare in Uttarakhand. Our mission is to provide affordable, high-quality medical care to all sections of society.

With a team of highly qualified doctors, nurses and support staff, we offer comprehensive healthcare services across multiple specialties. Our hospital is equipped with modern medical technology and follows international standards of patient care.

We believe in treating not just the illness, but the whole person - with compassion, respect and dedication to excellence in healthcare delivery.
